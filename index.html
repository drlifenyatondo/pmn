About Me

I am Purity Mufarowashe Nyatondo, a 22-year-old HIV, SRHR, and Mental Health Advocate from Zimbabwe dedicated to transforming the HIV response through peer-led support, stigma reduction, and mental health integration. As a Peer Supporter at Zvandiri, with the READY Movement, and as Communications Officer at Mental Health Sustainability, I lead strategic communications, craft empowering narratives, and create safe spaces for adolescents living with HIV.

My journey has taken me from the REPSSI Psychosocial Support Forum in Johannesburg, to ICASA 2023 in Harare, to AIDS2024 in Munich where I was part of the IAS Young Leaders Program, and most recently to the 9th International Workshop on Adolescence, SRHR, and HIV in Gaborone. In 2025, I proudly hosted the IAS Youth Hub Workshop, bringing together young voices to shape the future of HIV and mental health advocacy.

I believe the sky is the target, not the limit—because targets are meant to be surpassed. My vision is to move beyond surviving to thriving, and to continue building the READY to Thrive movement.
